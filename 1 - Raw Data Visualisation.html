    #flip image

    df = df.reindex(columns=cols)

Raw Data¶

Our dataframe is a composed of rows containing the 64 (8x8) thermal sensor Pixels of the Grid-EYE.
with column P0 - P7 the top line of pixels, and P56 - P63 the bottom line.
Each value therefore corresponds to a temperature in oC
Each row is timestamped

You might have noticed how noisy the data is. There are two main reasons for this.

  1. The accuracy and precision of the Grideye is low (±2.5°C, and ±0.25°C respectively) [1](https://industrial.panasonic.com/cdbs/www-data/pdf/ADI8000/ADI8000C53.pdf)
  2. Temperature of air varies in a ventilated (HVAC) system, often more than the Grideye's precision [2](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0360132396000340)
